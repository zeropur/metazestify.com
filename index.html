GAME OVERVIEW

Arsonate is an innovative puzzle game that combines elements of logic, spatial reasoning, and creative problem-solving. Each level presents a unique challenge that will test your ability to think outside the box.

With its striking visual style and immersive audio design, Arsonate creates an engaging atmosphere that enhances the gameplay experience and keeps you coming back for more.

HOW TO PLAY

Use your mouse or touch controls to interact with the game environment. Each puzzle has its own set of rules and mechanics that you'll discover as you play.

The game gradually introduces new concepts and challenges, allowing you to build your skills as you progress through increasingly complex levels.
